About SokoConnect

Empowering Local Businesses Since 2024

Our Story

SokoConnect was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to connect local traders and businesses, creating a vibrant ecosystem where entrepreneurs can collaborate, support each other, and grow together.

In today's digital world, many small and medium-sized enterprises struggle to find reliable business partners and expand their networks. We saw this challenge and decided to build a platform that bridges this gap.

Our mission is to strengthen local economies by making it easier for businesses to connect, collaborate, and create opportunities for mutual growth.

Our Mission

To create a trustworthy platform that connects local businesses and traders, enabling them to collaborate, innovate, and achieve sustainable growth while strengthening their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every local business has access to the networks, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive, regardless of their size or location.
